Home page

Soul João

São João is one of the most popular festivals in Brazil, and Campina Grande is known for having the biggest São João in the world! During the month of June, the city transforms into one big stage for music, food, and fun. The streets are decorated with colorful flags and lights, and there are many events happening all over the city.

If you are planning to visit Campina Grande during São João, it is important to prepare in advance. The city gets very busy during the festival, so it is essential to have all the information you need to make the most of your visit. This is where the Soul João project comes in!

Soul João is a project developed to help tourists who want to experience the complete São João in Campina Grande. Our website is a reliable source of up-to-date information about the events happening in the city during the festival. From live shows to cultural presentations and traditional festivities, we keep you informed so you don't miss any opportunity to have fun.

Our goal is to facilitate the planning of your trip by providing all the essential information in one place. Through Soul João, you will have access to personalized itineraries, suggesting the best places to visit and the most interesting activities to participate in during the festival. Whether you are looking for live music, dancing, typical foods, or simply want to have fun in a festive atmosphere, Soul João has everything you need.

In summary, Soul João is your complete guide to making the most of São João in Campina Grande. With up-to-date information, valuable tips, and an active online community, we are here to make your experience unforgettable. Whether you are a local resident or a tourist from out of town, Soul João is by your side, accompanying you in every step of this exciting party. Get ready to dive into the culture and joy of São João in Campina Grande with us!

Partys

Campina Grande, the city known for hosting the largest São João in the world, offers a unique festive experience during this time of year. During the month of June, the parties in Campina Grande are at their peak, bringing joy, music and dance to the streets. The parties of São João in Campina Grande are known for their contagious and lively atmosphere. People gather to celebrate June traditions, wearing traditional costumes, such as the famous quadrilhas, with their colorful costumes and choreographed dances. Music is a central element of these festivals, with forró bands and local artists entertaining the public with their live performances.

The shows at the Parque do Povo in Campina Grande are known as one of the city's greatest highlights. During the month of June, when the traditional June festival takes place, the park is transformed into a real stage for great musical performances, attracting thousands of people from all over the country. The Parque do Povo is a large, wooded area, designed to house a complete structure for the Campina Grande June Festival. With a capacity to receive hundreds of thousands of people, the park is the stage for shows by renowned artists of Brazilian music, especially forró and sertanejo. During the festival days, the Parque do Povo is divided into sectors, such as the Main Stage, where the main shows take place, and the alternative stages, where local and regional artists perform. There is also a large food area, with stalls selling typical June food, such as corn, canjica, and pamonha, among others. The shows at Parque do Povo are marked by a lively and contagious atmosphere, with the public dancing to the sound of the music and participating in June quadrilhas (square dancing). It is common to see couples dancing forró, dressed in traditional costumes, and people of all ages having fun to the contagious rhythm of the party. Among the artists who have performed at Parque do Povo are names like Elba Ramalho, Wesley Safadão, Simone e Simaria, Gusttavo Lima, Xand Avião, and other great exponents of Brazilian music.

Tourist Spots

Os Tropeiros da Borborema is a tourist attraction located in Campina Grande, in Paraíba, Brazil. The site is a tribute to the old troopers, merchants who, in past centuries, traveled through the region transporting goods on donkeys and horses. The Tropeiros da Borborema monument is a sculpture depicting a muleteer mounted on his pack animal, carrying bales of cotton. The statue, made of bronze, represents the historical and cultural importance of these characters in the region.

The Açude Velho is one of the most famous tourist attractions in Campina Grande, located in the state of Paraíba, Brazil. It is an artificial lake that has become an important historical and cultural landmark of the city. Built in 1865, the Açude Velho was initially designed to supply the city of Campina Grande with drinking water. Over time, it became a place of coexistence and leisure for the local residents, and today it is one of the main tourist attractions in the region. The lake has an extensive green area around it, with beautiful gardens, benches, and spaces for walks and physical exercise. It is a place frequented by families, couples, and friends who wish to enjoy a peaceful and pleasant environment. Besides its natural beauty, the Açude Velho also hosts several cultural events throughout the year, such as musical concerts, handicraft fairs, and gastronomic festivals. During the traditional Campina Grande June Festival, the lake becomes the stage for quadrilha presentations, attracting tourists from all over the country.
